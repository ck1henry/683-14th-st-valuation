Why is the retail area excluded from the tax document? 點解稅務文件冇計到地舖面積？

According to the SF Planning Code, this discrepancy is intentional: 簡單嚟講，呢條 三藩市規劃法例 (SF Planning Code) 正好解釋咗點解地舖面積冇計落去：

  • Legal Definition: The code strictly separates "residential" and "commercial" spaces. The official "Living Area" only calculates the upper residential floors. 法律定義唔同： 法例將「住宅」同「商業」空間嚴格區分。官方定義嘅「居住面積」(Living Area) 只會計算真正用嚟住嘅地方（即係上面嗰兩層住宅）。
  • Policy Exemption: To encourage neighborhood retail, the city often excludes ground-floor retail when calculating Floor Area Ratio (FAR) limits. 政府政策豁免： 為咗鼓勵區內有更多街舖，政府喺計算建築總面積限制（地積比率 FAR）嗰陣，通常會將地舖面積剔除。
  • Tax System Mechanics: Because Planning legally separates these uses, the Assessor's system automatically pulls only the "residential" figure (2,815 sq ft) and ignores the retail floor. 稅務系統設定： 因為規劃署喺法律上分開咗呢兩種用途，估價署嘅報稅電腦系統就只會抽取並顯示屬於「住宅」嘅數字（即係 2,815 平方呎），而自動忽略咗地下嗰層舖頭。

In summary, the official records aren't missing square footage; the city's legal and tax frameworks intentionally treat commercial retail and residential areas separately. 總括而言，官方文件上嘅數字並唔係計漏咗，而係三藩市嘅法律同稅務系統本身就刻意將商業地舖同住宅面積分開處理。

1. Independent Income Approach (Market Rents)1. 獨立收益法 (基於市場租金)

The Investment Method has been assessed entirely independently of the sales comparables, strictly utilising prevailing market rental evidence. The property operates as a mixed-use unified structure. Based on local submarket leasing data, we apply a stabilised market rent of $3.00/sq ft for the ground-floor retail unit and a conservative $4.80/sq ft for the unrenovated upper residential floors. Operating expenses are blended (5% for retail NNN, 35% for residential gross). At a market-led 5.75% capitalisation rate, this yields an underlying income value of approximately $2.37M.

收益法之評估完全獨立於銷售可比數據，嚴格採用現行市場租金證據。該物業作為住商混合之單一建築營運。根據本地子市場租賃數據，我們對地下商舖採用穩定的 $3.00/平方呎 市場租金，而針對未經翻新的樓上住宅樓層則保守估算為 $4.80/平方呎。營運開支採用混合計算（商舖 5%，住宅 35%）。以市場主導的 5.75% 資本化率計算，得出之基礎收益估值約為 237 萬美元。
