Valuer's Log: Chain of Thought

1. Search Strategy & Data Gathering

My initial search focused on "Mixed-Use" and "Multi-Family 2-4 Units" in the 94114 zip code (Castro/Upper Market) and 94103 (Mission/Duboce border). I prioritised transactions from Q3 2025 to present (Q1 2026) to reflect the post-recovery pricing stability. Key keywords: "Storefront," "Live/Work," and "Value Add."

2. Comparable Selection Logic

I filtered for properties with a blend of retail and residential.
• Comp 1 (450 Castro): Slightly superior location (Prime Castro), smaller sq ft. Higher PSF ($697) justified by location.
• Comp 2 (2100 Market): Larger building (5,200 sq ft), creating a quantum discount. Lower PSF ($596).
• Comp 3 (180 Church): Most similar in location (Transit heavy). $610 PSF.
Conclusion: Subject property (4,015 sq ft) sits centrally in the comparable set. I estimated a market PSF of ~$615-$625.
