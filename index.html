Valuer's Log: Title Adjustments估值師日誌：產權報告調整

1. Unit Configuration Impact1. 單位配置之影響

The Title Report explicitly lists "681-683-685 14th Street" on the Quit Claim Deed (Page 28). This confirms the 3-storey structure houses 3 distinct units. I have modelled this as 1 retail unit and 2 residential flats. This spreads the vacancy risk, allowing me to reduce the structural Vacancy assumption from 5% to 4%.

產權報告的放棄權利契約 (Quit Claim Deed，第28頁) 明確列出了「681-683-685 14th Street」。這確認了這座三層建築包含三個獨立單位 (3伙)。我已將模型設定為 1個商舖 和 2個住宅單位。這有效分散了空置風險，因此我將結構性空置率假設由 5% 下調至 4%。

2. Proposition 13 Tax Reassessment2. 加州第13號提案 (Prop 13) 稅項重估

Page 19 shows the property has a 2025 Assessed Value of $879,663 resulting in $13,164.70 in taxes. Because the family trust has owned it since 1991, they benefit heavily from Prop 13. A buyer acquiring this asset at $2.43M will be reassessed at ~1.2% of the purchase price, creating a new tax burden of roughly $29,160. My revised OpEx model assumes 32% expenses to account for this massive impending tax hike.

報告第19頁顯示該物業2025年的評估價值為 $879,663 美元，應繳稅項為 $13,164.70 美元。由於家族信託自1991年起持有該物業，他們享受了 Prop 13 的極大稅務優惠。若新買家以 $243萬美元收購物業，稅基將重新評估為買價的約1.2%，產生每年約 $29,160 美元的新稅務負擔。我修改後的營運開支 (OpEx) 模型已將總開支比率設為32%，以應對這筆大幅增加的預期稅款。
