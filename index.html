1. Configuration & The "Demised Premium"1. 單位配置與「分契溢價」

The dataset highlights a severe pricing bifurcation in the broader market. Individually demised Condo/Flat units command massive premiums, routinely trading between $900 - $1,300 per sq ft. The subject property, however, is a unified 3-storey structure. As such, these condo comps must be heavily discounted or excluded.

數據庫顯示本地市場的定價出現嚴重兩極化。獨立分契的公寓/單位享有極高的溢價，常以每平方呎 $900 至 $1,300 美元成交。然而，目標物業為一棟統一的三層建築。因此，這些公寓可比數據必須大幅折讓或剔除。

2. Identifying the Golden Comps2. 鎖定「黃金可比交易」

The most directly relevant sales are the Multi-Family / Unified blocks. 178-180 Church St serves as the anchor comparable: it is nearly identical in size (4,100 sq ft vs our 4,015 sq ft) and traded at $609/sf. 32 Landers St (2,600 sq ft) confirms this baseline at $625/sf. Applying these weights, I establish a baseline value of $605/sf for the subject property.

最直接相關的銷售數據是多戶型/統一建築。178-180 Church St 作為核心可比物業：其面積與目標物業幾乎相同（4,100 平方呎 vs 4,015 平方呎），成交價為 $609/呎。32 Landers St (2,600 平方呎) 以 $625/呎 的價格確認了這個基準。綜合加權後，我將目標物業的基準價值定為 $605/呎。
